Now that you understand your Weton "your daily energetic signature" we can explore a much deeper layer of your blueprint: your **Wuku**.

If your Weton is a snapshot of your personality, your Wuku is the epic myth that your soul was born into. It is the sacred story and spiritual context of your birth week.

The Javanese Pawukon calendar is a 210-day cycle, which is divided into 30 different "weeks" or chapters. Each of these 30 chapters is a Wuku, and each one lasts for 7 days. Your birth date falls within one of these 30 Wuku, and its unique story profoundly influences your character, your destiny, and your life's purpose.

Think of it like this:

- **Western Zodiac:** You are one of 12 signs in a 365-day cycle.
- **Weton:** You are one of 35 archetypes in a 35-day cycle.
- **Wuku:** You are born into one of 30 sacred stories in a 210-day cycle.

Each Wuku has its own set of symbolic patrons, a guardian deity, a tree, and a bird that act as your spiritual guides. Discovering your Wuku is like finding the lost chapter of your personal mythology.

### The 30 Wuku and Their Key Traits

- **Sinta**<br>
  Sinta individuals possess sharp minds and are natural magnets for opportunity, often becoming a source of support due to their empathetic and forgiving nature. Despite their calm demeanor, they can be stubborn and passionate, yet remain dynamic and efficient in getting things done.<br><br>
- **Landep**<br>
  Landep individuals are sharp, quick-thinking problem solvers with a charismatic and well-liked presence. While skilled and generous, they can be stubborn and their instructions quite firm, sometimes forgetting to express gratitude.<br><br>
- **Wukir**<br>
  Those born under Wukir embody stability and strength, aiming high in life with grace and politeness. They possess natural leadership qualities, an appealing demeanor, and are seen as reliable and generous figures.<br><br>
- **Kurantil**<br>
  Kurantil individuals are lively and creative, with a knack for capturing attention, though this energy can manifest as a quick temper. Their strong drive and diligence often lead to significant success, but they should be mindful of transparency in their dealings.<br><br>
- **Tolu**<br>
  People under Tolu are patient and possess strategic, calculating minds well-suited for precision work. However, they must be mindful of potential tendencies toward arrogance, fickleness, and indirect communication.<br><br>
- **Gumbreng**<br>
  Gumbreng individuals are naturally generous and helpful, often blessed with good fortune. They balance a strong, determined willpower with an affectionate and caring personality, making them both driven and warm-hearted.<br><br>
- **Warigalit**<br>
  Warigalit individuals are dynamic, expressive communicators with an attractive presence and a love for romance. Their sensitive hearts make them easily moved and prone to jealousy, with their deep emotions being a key part of their personality.<br><br>
- **Warigagung**<br>
  Warigagung individuals are natural leaders, hardworking and capable of shouldering great responsibility to achieve their ambitions. They are valued for being true to their word but can be intense when angered, showing great resilience in the face of setbacks.<br><br>
- **Julungwangi**<br>
  Julungwangi individuals are often considered lucky and adventurous, with a strong sense of pride that dislikes being overshadowed. They are generous and charismatic, which makes them popular, though they appreciate recognition for their kindness.<br><br>
- **Sungsang**<br>
  Sungsang individuals may struggle with suspicion and moodiness, sometimes being demanding or critical. Despite these challenges and a need for their generosity to be acknowledged, they are often blessed with good fortune.<br><br>
- **Galungan**<br>
  Galungan individuals are friendly, hardworking, and emotionally strong, with a powerful moral compass driving them to do good. Their great compassion and charity often take priority over accumulating personal wealth.<br><br>
- **Kuningan**<br>
  Kuningan individuals often achieve high status and possess a spiritual or aesthetic beauty, enjoying solitude and meticulous habits. While talkative, they should be mindful of a tendency to exaggerate or be less than factual.<br><br>
- **Langkir**<br>
  Langkir individuals are often intelligent with finances but grapple with intense passions that can be self-destructive. Their challenging nature can make them resistant to rules and quick to anger, leading to more obstacles in life.<br><br>
- **Mandasiya**<br>
  Mandasiya individuals are characterized by a stubborn, strong will and can be quick-tempered and unforgiving when angered. Though they may appear patient, managing their deep-seated and harsh temper is a key life challenge.<br><br>
- **Julungpujut**<br>
  Julungpujut individuals are social, well-liked, and ambitious, aspiring to leadership and disliking being second best. While they can be easily influenced, their drive ensures they secure enough resources for a comfortable life.<br><br>
- **Pahang**<br>
  Pahang individuals are wise and articulate but can be impatient, hot-tempered, and prone to harsh speech or dishonesty. A contrast between their outer appearance and inner state can lead to arrogance and frequent conflict.<br><br>
- **Kuruwelut**<br>
  Kuruwelut individuals are loyal, responsible, and intellectually sharp, known for their noble character and integrity. Though reserved in speech, their respected judgment makes them a source of stability, and they may enjoy displaying their successes.<br><br>
- **Marakeh**<br>
  Marakeh individuals are brave, firm, and capable, with a strong character that dislikes being surpassed. They are patient, compassionate, and careful with money, though they may sometimes boast about their good deeds.<br><br>
- **Tambir**<br>
  Tambir individuals are creative and innovative, often showing a different face to the world than their inner self. They are confident speakers whose words are impactful but can also be sharp, and they enjoy sharing exclusive knowledge.<br><br>
- **Mandangkungan**<br>
  Those born under Mandangkungan are eloquent speakers who are naturally skilled at social interaction and building trust. They are known for being faithful, steadfast, and reliable, which helps them foster strong relationships.<br><br>
- **Maktal**<br>
  Maktal individuals are ambitious, hardworking, and admired for their gentle speech and effective work. Beneath their calm and solitary exterior lies a strong individualistic streak, leading to a life of potential ups and downs.<br><br>
- **Wuye**<br>
  Wuye individuals bring joy to others with their direct and surprising communication, though they can have a quick but fleeting temper. They are ambitious and appreciate life's pleasures, but may face a challenging path or inner struggles despite their strong heart.<br><br>
- **Manail**<br>
  Manail individuals are ambitious, sharp-minded strategists who dislike inactivity and are always vigilant. While socially engaging, they can appear arrogant or defensive, and their commands may lack authoritative weight.<br><br>
- **Prangbakat**<br>
  Prangbakat individuals are brave and enjoy challenges, showing warrior-like drive and efficiency in achieving their goals. Despite a potential shyness and quick temper, they are generous and compassionate leaders whose tone can shift when giving directions.<br><br>
- **Bala**<br>
  Bala individuals are strong, resilient, and fearless, but must be mindful of a tendency towards causing chaos or mischief. While linked to potentially destructive forces, they can mitigate these traits and achieve success.<br><br>
- **Wugu**<br>
  Wugu individuals are clever, quick learners with a simple and humble character, often blessed with good fortune. They prefer quiet environments and are generally good-natured, though they can sometimes experience feelings of jealousy.<br><br>
- **Wayang**<br>
  Wayang individuals are intelligent, authoritative, and often achieve prosperity through their generous and meticulous nature. They are patient and well-liked, though they appreciate recognition for their kindness and their commands can become more demanding.<br><br>
- **Kulawu**<br>
  Kulawu individuals are seen as wise and just, with a strong character, high aspirations, and a calm demeanor. Following a potentially challenging youth, their intuitive wisdom often leads them to great happiness and fortune later in life.<br><br>
- **Dukut**<br>
  Dukut individuals are diligent, sharp-minded, and skillful workers with a strong character and many friends. They are driven by a desire for material possessions and may harbor inner fears, sometimes exhibiting arrogance.<br><br>
- **Watugunung**<br>
  Watugunung individuals are gentle and patient, embodying a duality between spiritual contemplation and worldly pleasures. They possess a high character but prefer solitude, sometimes appearing arrogant, and are prone to jealousy despite their bright inner spirit.<br>
